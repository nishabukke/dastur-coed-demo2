Home Uniform
The Schoolendeavours to inculcate habits of cleanliness and hygiene. Parents are therefore, requested to see that their children are always smartly dressed, not only to school but also, outside school and at public functions.

General Instructions

  • Uniform should be tailor made to fit the child decently as per his / her size.
  • School uniform socks should be ankle length and white in colour. There should be no logo / motif / emblem on it.
  • Students to wear ONLY dark blue canvas shoes with dark blue laces.
  • Students to wear a plain navy-blue pullover during winters with a V-neck and long sleeves. The pullover must have the school’s name and school logo embroidered on it.
  • Nails should be clipped short and kept clean at all times.
  • School uniforms should be clean, neat and properly ironed each day.

Rules for Girls Uniform

  • Pinafore : The girls uniform should be a single pinafore made of spun material with blue pinstripes on a white base. The sleeves should have a dark blue band on it. A white slip / inner should be worn under the uniform
  • Belt : A dark blue belt of interwoven material should be worn around the waist, the length of which should not exceed the length of the uniform.
  • Tie : The school tie should have the school emblem as per size, and should be below till the ribcage of the child.
  • Hair : Girls should have their hair neatly tied in two plaits with a middle parting. Each plait should be tied with a dark blue satin ribbon. A dark blue hair band should be used to keep all the hair neatly and from falling on to the face.
  • For P.T. :
    • The P.T. T-shirt should be white with 3 bands of the house colour. Position of the emblem should be printed just above the hem on the sleeve.
    • The Skirts (skirt with shorts attached from inside) has to be dark navy blue in colour in spun material. The length of the skorts has to be up to the knees.

Uniform

Rules for Boys Uniform

  • Shirt : The material used for the boys shirt is spun material with blue pin stripes on a white base. The pocket on the left side of the shirt must have a dark blue band on it. A white vest must be worn under the shirt.
  • Trousers : The trouser should be made of dark blue terry cot material. There should be two pleats on each side of the trouser. Narrowed and tight pants are strictly not allowed.
  • Belt : A dark blue belt as per the size of the student’s waist.
  • Tie : The school tie should be 5 cms to 7 cms wide for small to big respectively. Position of the school emblem should approximately be 6 inches above the tip of the tie. The tie should reach the lower end of the rib cage.
  • Hair : Boys must cut their hair short - not more than ½ an inch on the back and sides of the head, and one inch on the top and crown. Fancy haircuts like Mushroom cut, Quiff hair style, Spiky cuts, etc. are not acceptable. No other hair styles are acceptable. Sikh students may only use dark blue turbans.
  • For P.T. :
    • The P.T. T-shirt should be white with 3 bands of the house colour. Position of the emblem should be printed just above the hem on the sleeve.
    • Shorts should be dark navy blue in colour. A pocket should be made on the right side of the shorts. A white piping should be there on both sides of the shorts along the stitch. Position of the emblem should be embroidered on the shorts.
